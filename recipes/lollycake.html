Lolly Cake

A classic kiwi favourite using Griffin's Malt biscuits.

Ingredients

  • 120g Butter
  • Â½ tin of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 packet of Pascall Explorers or Fruit Puff lollies
  • 1 Packet of Malt biscuits - crushed
  • Desiccated coconut

Steps

  1. Warm butter and condensed milk in microwave.
  2. Cut Explorers or Fruit Puffs into bite sized chunks
  3. Mix butter, condensed milk, crushed Malt biscuits and lollies together.
  4. Form into a log shape. Roll in coconut
  5. When set, cut into slices and serve
